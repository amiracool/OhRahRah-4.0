Welcome to OhRahRah

OhRahRah was born from a love for animals and a desire to provide gentle, personalised grooming. Every session is crafted with patience and respect, ensuring each dog feels pampered and at ease.

Hi, I’m Amira!

I'm the heart and hands behind OhRahRah. With years of experience and a lifelong love for dogs, my goal is to make every pup feel special. I believe grooming should be a positive experience, and I work one-on-one with each dog to ensure a calm and enjoyable session.

Whether it’s a simple trim or a luxurious spa treatment, I treat every pet like family, giving them the attention and care they deserve.

Our Fur-losophy

  • Gentleness - Treating every dog with patience and care.
  • Quality - Using only the best products for safe and effective grooming.
  • Personalisation - Tailoring each groom to fit the unique needs of each pet.

Why Choose OhRahRah?

OhRahRah is dedicated to providing a grooming experience that’s as unique as your pet. Our one-on-one, home-based setup allows for a stress-free environment where pets feel safe and cared for. With luxury products and a commitment to each pet's well-being, OhRahRah goes beyond grooming—it's a pampering experience.
